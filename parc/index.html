reelyActive Parc

Our home and living lab from 2020 to 2024.

Tell me more

Located at the corner of Sherbrooke and Parc in Montréal, our Park Avenue Research Centre was ambitiously named in honour of another PARC—one which was instrumental in creating a trillion dollar computing industry.

reelyActive Parc was a living lab pioneering a future in which occupants of any space could exist and interact both physically and digitally, at a human scale and with a human-centric focus.
