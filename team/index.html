Team

Who we are makes us what we are

Active team

Jeffrey

Co-founder and CEO

2012-present

I co-founded reelyActive to make real-time location technologies broadly accessible. At the same time, Internet of Things adoption accelerated, encouraging me to expand this vision to a world where every physical space becomes location-and-context-aware. My objective is to contribute significantly to the sustainable advancement of humanity, and I am convinced that the foundational technologies we develop at reelyActive contribute significantly to this objective.

Front-line and full-stack best describes my unique qualifications, the result of nearly two decades of startup experience, having worn every hat imaginable, working across industries and borders. My computer engineering background has afforded me the rare opportunity to create RFID and RTLS technologies from the ground up, with my entrepreneurial drive providing the courage to commercialise and evangelise their mass adoption.

Visit reelyActive's blog to read my 90+ contributions since 2012, including our annual April Fool's post tradition! I'm also proud to contribute reelyActive's bibliography.
Benoît

Chief Operating Officer

2015-present

While technology, expertise, vision, values and business opportunities attracted me to joining reelyActive, I have been particularly captivated by the "open architecture" culture in place, which we have expanded over the years to how we work and build relationships with our customers and trading partners, by leveraging principles generally associated with software design. Our way of doing business and structuring value networks align favorably with my conception of the ideal ecosystems architecture: lean, modular, open, fluid and adaptable structure governed by policies fostering collaboration and innovation while nurturing organizational agility.

Human-centric transformational leader, customer oriented intrapreneur and business ecosystem architect with over 25 years in the high tech industry, specifically in hardware product development and manufacturing.

I developed reelyActive's DEEM approach to digital transformation.
Camille

Client Success Lead

2019-present

Thrilled by new technologies, I am convinced that open source fosters innovation. Open source might have a significant impact on the earth itself, as more and more developers participate in the development of something that can change our future for good. This brought me to reelyActive who's nurturing this philosophy with its open source technology and culture.

Graduated from an engineering school specializing in the Internet of Things, I joined reelyActive in 2019 as a junior project manager working on several client projects. My ability to empathize and maintain long-term relationships of trust with our clients led me to become the Client Success Lead of reelyActive.

Follow La transformation numérique du Québec on LinkedIn for my insights on digital transformation.
