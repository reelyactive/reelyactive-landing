Content Storyteller

Execute our "continuous content improvement" strategy

Our challenge: too many stories to tell, too little time to produce, curate and communicate them all!

Your challenge: efficiently creating content on a weekly cycle to tell the story of our Pareto Anywhere platform to existing and prospective users, including business-minded and tech-minded audiences alike.

Imagine explaining the Internet before the Internet. Or web search before Google. Or how the Internet of Things enables physical spaces to become searchable like the web. Oh wait, that last one is what you'll be challenged to achieve as reelyActive's Content Storyteller!

Storytelling content includes:

  • Case Studies
  • Explainer Videos
  • Product Presentations
  • White Papers
  • Creative Media (aka anything!)

You'll undoubtedly have:

  • Impeccable written English skills
  • Experience with web, image and video editing
  • Comfort with dynamic work environments
  • A penchant for prolific efficiency
  • A calling for clever creativity (and ❤ alliterations)

This is a full time position which will involve a mix of work-from-home and work-from-Parc, the latter which we expect you'll enjoy tapping into as a source of inspiration.
