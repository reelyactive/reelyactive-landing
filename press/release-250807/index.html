Minew and reelyActive: Unlocking Scalable, Open IoT with Seamless Hardware–Software Integration

Shenzhen, China & Montréal, Canada — August 7th, 2025.   Minew and reelyActive are proud to announce an expanded collaboration that builds on years of trusted partnership. By combining Minew's proven portfolio of IoT devices with reelyActive's powerful middleware platform, Pareto Anywhere, we're helping businesses move faster—from sensor to insight, across industries.

A Stronger IoT Foundation: Hardware Meets Middleware

Minew is a global leader in IoT hardware, offering a wide range of BLE tags and sensors designed for real-world reliability and scalability. From environmental monitoring to asset and personnel management, Minew's devices are trusted by integrators and enterprises worldwide.

reelyActive, based in Montréal, develops open-source software that translates wireless signals into actionable data. Its flagship product, Pareto Anywhere, is a lightweight, application-agnostic platform that standardizes data across multiple wireless protocols and vendors.

Together, the two technologies form a robust end-to-end solution:

Minew offers sensors, personnel tags, asset trackers, and gateways that deliver real-time intelligence on location, movement, identity, and environmental conditions.

Minew | IoT Devices Innovator and Manufacturer

Pareto Anywhere is an open-source middleware platform developed by reelyActive. It translates real-time wireless signals, including Bluetooth® LE, RAIN RFID, EnOcean Alliance, and Wi-Fi, into a unified data format. Designed to support current and future wireless technologies, Pareto Anywhere enables seamless integration across mixed infrastructures and device vendors. By delivering clean, standardized data to any application stack, it empowers developers to build flexible, scalable IoT systems without proprietary lock-in.

Pareto Anywhere by reelyActive

Real-World Applications: Minew Devices in Action with Pareto Anywhere

This partnership is live and working. A range of Minew's wireless devices and gateways have already been successfully integrated with Pareto Anywhere, delivering real-time, structured data streams that are both human-readable and machine-ready.

MST01 — Industrial Temperature & Humidity Sensor

Built for harsh industrial environments, MST01 provides highly accurate environmental readings. With large local data storage and customizable thresholds, it ensures no data is lost. Paired with Pareto Anywhere, temperature and humidity data streams can be directed into monitoring dashboards or analytics engines in real time.

MSR01 — Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor

The MSR01 leverages 60GHz mmWave technology to detect presence, count people, and monitor flow with 99% accuracy, anonymously. When connected through Pareto Anywhere, real-time occupancy data becomes instantly available for space optimization, energy management, or safety enforcement.

MSA01 — Ambient Light Sensor

Designed for precise lux measurement, MSA01 broadcasts real-time light level data via BLE. Pareto Anywhere processes this data and feeds it into any building management system without custom coding.

What this Partnership Brings to Customers

  • Fast, flexible integration with no vendor lock-in
  • Unified data stream across multiple technologies and gateways
  • Freedom to deploy on any infrastructure—cloud, edge or local
  • Lower deployment and development costs thanks to plug-and-play compatibility
  • Support for diverse use cases, from smart buildings and warehouses to healthcare, retail, and research environments

For organizations juggling different device brands, wireless protocols, and software stacks, this partnership removes complexity and speeds up time to value. Customers can combine Minew hardware with other hardware ecosystems, and still receive standardized, ready-to-use data via Pareto Anywhere.

"We have long appreciated Minew wireless devices and gateways for their quality, variety and unbeatable value. Our partnership with Minew affords organisations around the globe an efficient IoT adoption path from proof of concept to deployment at scale," said Jeffrey Dungen, co-founder and CEO of reelyActive.

“Our partnership with reelyActive gives Minew devices an open, software-ready path into any system architecture. It enables faster deployments and makes IoT integration more accessible for everyone,” said the Minew team.

About Minew

Minew (stock code: 872374) is a trusted wireless technology innovator and a reliable loT device manufacturer founded in 2007. The company specializes in Bluetooth, LoRaWAN, Cellular (LTE/NB-IoT/4G/5G), Wi-Fi, UWB, GPS, GNSS and satellite communication technologies, offering OEM/ODM/JDM services for beacons, asset/personnel trackers, sensors, gateways & FindMy tags. With 10,000+m² smart factories, full-stack R&D, and 300M+ devices shipped globally, we empower industries with real-time visibility & smart automation.

About reelyActive

reelyActive stewards the open source technologies that are foundational to a truly interoperable Internet of Things (IoT). Founded in Montréal, Canada in 2012, we are leaders in ambient identification, sensing and real-time location (RTLS) using any combination of wireless devices and gateway infrastructure. Our Pareto Anywhere open source IoT middleware is used in production by startups, researchers and Fortune 500 companies alike, all around the world. Integrators and end users may leverage reelyActive's team for impartial guidance in realising IoT solutions that they can embrace as their own.
