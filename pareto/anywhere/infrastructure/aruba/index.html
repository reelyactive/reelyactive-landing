Aruba Networks Integration

Realise the potential of physical/digital convergence using the network infrastructure that's already in your space.

Aruba access points can forward ambient Bluetooth Low Energy packets to Pareto Anywhere in order to make the physical spaces they cover context-aware for any application.

This functionality is available as of Aruba Instant version 8.8
