Minew Devices

Wireless sensors and beacons that transmit data for Pareto Anywhere to make sense of.

Tell me more

Wirelessly monitor environmental conditions, assets and personnel using the connectivity infrastructure and software stack of your choice.

Minew devices transmit identifiers and sensor data.

Pareto Anywhere middleware affords you the freedom to choose:

  • any Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity infrastructure,
  • any software stack,

processing and relaying the data between the two, in a standard format that makes sense.
