ELA Innovation

Ultra-rugged Bluetooth beacons and IoT sensors that transmit data for Pareto Anywhere to make sense of.

Tell me more

Wirelessly monitor physical spaces and assets using the connectivity infrastructure and analytics software of your choice.

ELA Innovation devices transmit identifiers and sensor data.

Pareto Anywhere middleware affords you the freedom to choose:

  • any Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity infrastructure,
  • any data analytics software,

processing and relaying the data between the two, in a standard format that makes sense.
