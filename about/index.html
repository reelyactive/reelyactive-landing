About Us

The who/what is where/how of reelyActive.

A brief history

Founded in Montréal in 2012 with a goal to "create the first simple and accessible cloud-based active RFID system," reelyActive has since charted out a pioneering course towards an optimistic future where the human experience and societal efficiency are harmoniously, sustainably and ethically elevated by technology.

Today, in 2020, we continue to be a purpose-driven, evolutionary (teal) organisation which proudly embraces open source development, coopetition, activism and emergence.
