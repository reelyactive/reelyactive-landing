Context

Here's a reely brief summary.

reelyActive stewards the open source technologies that are foundational to a truly interoperable Internet of Things (IoT). We are leaders in ambient identification, sensing and real-time location (RTLS) using any combination of wireless devices and gateway infrastructure.

Our Pareto Anywhere open source IoT middleware is used in production by startups, researchers and Fortune 500 companies alike, all around the world. Integrators and end users can leverage our team for impartial guidance in realising IoT solutions that they can embrace as their own.
